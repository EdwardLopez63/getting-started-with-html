Thinking Like A Programmer

Steps

1.Procedural Thinking

It involves taking a problem and breaking it down into steps. Making an organized list will help.

2. Abstract Thinking

Finding connection in stuff that you might not understand at first. I believe just by looking at the html tags, I was able to make connections I didn't fully understand.

3. Systematic Debugging

Ability to find the problems and figure out the possible causes of the problem. Kind of like when a new computer hardware configuration doesn't work. (Example: Messed up RAM in my computer)

4. Technological Empathy

Trying to understand how the computer sees things. Italics and boldness isn't just there automatically. HTML is key.

5. Systems Thinkings

Taking a big idea and breaking it down just like this list. The topic being "Thinking Like A Programmer".
